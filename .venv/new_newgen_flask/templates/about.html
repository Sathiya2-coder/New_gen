About NEC NewGen

Pioneering Engineering Education Since 1994

National Engineering College

Established in 1994, National Engineering College (NEC) has been at the forefront of engineering education in Tamil Nadu. Located in Kovilpatti, our institution has consistently produced world-class engineers who have made significant contributions to various industries globally.

Our Mission

To impart quality technical education and foster innovation, research, and entrepreneurship among students, preparing them to become globally competent engineers and responsible citizens.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education and research, creating professionals who can lead and innovate in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Our Values

Excellence, Innovation, Integrity, Collaboration, and Social Responsibility form the core values that guide every aspect of our institution's functioning.

NEC NewGen Initiative

The NewGen initiative represents our commitment to staying at the cutting edge of technology. Through this program, we focus on emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, IoT, Cloud Computing, and Blockchain, ensuring our students are industry-ready for the future.
